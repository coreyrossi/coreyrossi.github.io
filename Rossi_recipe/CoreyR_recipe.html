Asparagus Pesto

This is the best recipe you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Asparagus
  • Pine Nuts
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Lemon Zest

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Place pasta in water
  3. Blend other ingredients
  4. Remove pasta from water
  5. Plate pasta then top with blended ingredients
